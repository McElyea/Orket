# Requirement Spec

## Scope
Define deletion policy for local audit records.

## Definitions
- Retained delete: remove after retention window.
- Hold exception: legal hold pauses deletion.

## Non-goals
- No remote archival.

## Invariants
- Local-only storage.
- Single deletion policy is enforced.
- Legal hold pathway is explicit.

## Failure Codes
- REQ_POLICY_CONTRADICTION
- REQ_HOLD_UNDEFINED

## Acceptance Tests
- Verify deletion behavior is deterministic.
- Verify 30-day retention before deletion.
- Verify legal hold pauses deletion.
