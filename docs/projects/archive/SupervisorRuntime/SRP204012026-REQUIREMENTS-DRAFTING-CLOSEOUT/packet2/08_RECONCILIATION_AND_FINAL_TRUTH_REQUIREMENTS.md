# Reconciliation And Final Truth Requirements
Last updated: 2026-04-01
Status: Completed archived draft packet requirements authority
Owner: Orket Core
Packet role: Stage 3

## Purpose

Define divergence-handling and terminal closure authority for Packet 2.

## Draft requirements

1. Packet 2 must promote reconciliation into one explicit authority family over divergence and safe continuation or stop outcomes.
2. Every governed terminal path must converge on one final-truth authority family.
3. Final truth must derive from published runtime and control-plane records rather than from summaries acting as alternate authority.
4. Reconciliation, recovery, effect, and operator inputs must remain visible in terminal truth where they materially influenced closure.
5. Packet 2 must reject false-green completion claims when final truth cannot be justified from published authority.

## Non-goals

1. human-readable narrative reporting
2. broad incident-management workflow design
3. replacing all summary projections
